About

Our Mission

Hydration for Everyone—Funded by Sponsors

We started H2OLL with a simple belief: access to clean, refreshing water should never depend on your wallet. So we turned the humble bottle into a sponsor-powered media channel. Brands fund the water; communities drink for free.

Each bottle carries a full-wrap label and a unique QR code that connects people to sponsor campaigns, offers, or causes. It’s real-world reach with measurable engagement—and a feel-good moment in every hand.

How It Works

Simple, Transparent, Impactful

1) Sponsor

Brands choose a package and share their message and campaign goals.

2) Design & Print

We craft the label, add a unique QR, and print compliant, eco-minded packaging.

3) Distribute

Our team handles targeted, high-footfall drops at events, campuses, gyms, and more.

4) Measure

Live dashboards show scans, CTR, and impact—then we optimize together.
